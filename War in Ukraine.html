08.03.2022
16:54

In Dnipro region, in the village Hryhorivka,  a russian missile hit a school stadium. The school is housing 30 refugees from a nearby town. No casualties reported so far.

On the 8th of March, on the N-14 highway Russian troops opened fire on a minibus transporting teachers of an orphanage to their workplace. 3 people died (preliminary reports).

Russian troops continue to block humanitarian corridors, shell residential/social infrastructure and amass troops in Makariv, Irpin, Hostomel and other cities in Kyiv region. – Head of Kyiv Military Administration

Two people died under Russian shelling as they were trying to feed the animals left in the Kharkiv eco-park. – reports local media (Suspilne news)

In Mariupol, a 6-year-old girl has died from dehydration. The emergency services retrieved her from under debris. It is not clear how long the child stayed there. The girl’s mother has died. – reports local media (Suspilne)

Singer Olena with a weapon in her hands defends her hometown of Zhytomyr

On the first day of the war, when Russia started bombing Ukraine’s regional centers, Olena, her husband, and their friends built checkpoints around Zhytomyr. They enlisted in the Territorial Defense Forces to help with medicines and organizing humanitarian aid. Now Olena has her own weapon and actively helps her unit.

She says that everyone’s morale is high because they stand for their homeland.

Kyivan chef Mykhailo feeds refugees in Transcarpathia for free

Volunteers in Uzhhorod turned a local restaurant into a place where you can eat for free as a break during the three-to-four-day trip to the border. Mykhailo volunteers here among other chefs. Internationally acclaimed ethnographer Anna patrols the streets of Kyiv nonstop

Since the very first day of the war, Anna Senik has been safeguarding peace in the capital side by side with her comrades. She conducts reconnaissance activities, observing and patrolling the streets with a weapon in her hands. Even though she has never been armed before and hasn’t had any serious physical training, she works virtually round-the-clock. After all, this is her home district, where she was born and raised. It needs protection.
